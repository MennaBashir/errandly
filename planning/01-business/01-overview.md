# 📘 Product Overview & Executive Summary
### Project Codename: **Errandly** — On-Site Physical Freelancing Marketplace

> **Document Owner:** Product / CTO
> **Status:** v1.0 — MVP Definition
> **Stack:** React Native (Expo) · Node.js (NestJS) · PostgreSQL · Web Admin (Next.js)

---

## Value Proposition (one sentence)
**Errandly is an on-demand, location-based marketplace that connects "Masters" who need real-world physical errands done with verified "Runners" who execute them on-site, secured end-to-end by an escrow-protected payment system that releases funds only after the task is approved.**

## Elevator Pitch
Think *Uber* meets *TaskRabbit* but purpose-built for **physical micro-tasks and errands** — standing in government queues, hospital lines, picking up documents, finding a specific physical item in a market, manual paperwork. Masters fund the task into escrow before a Runner starts; Runners earn flexible income without needing digital skills; the platform takes a **10% commission** on completion.

## Revenue Model
Pay-as-you-go. The platform charges a **10% commission** on the total task cost, captured automatically on completion.

## Business Objectives (MVP)
| # | Objective | KPI / Success Metric |
|---|-----------|----------------------|
| 1 | Validate the core task loop | ≥ 60% of posted tasks reach `COMPLETED` |
| 2 | Build trust via escrow | < 5% dispute rate on completed tasks |
| 3 | Drive Runner liquidity | Avg. time-to-accept < 10 min in active zones |
| 4 | Monetize reliably | 100% of completed tasks auto-deduct 10% fee |
| 5 | Ensure safety | 100% of Runners pass KYC before first payout |

## Out of Scope (MVP)
- Multi-currency / cross-border payouts
- In-app video calls
- Subscription tiers for Masters
- Runner teams / sub-contracting
- Advanced ML dispute auto-resolution (rule-based only in MVP)
